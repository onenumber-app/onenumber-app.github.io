Request Account & Data Deletion

If you would like to request deletion of your OneNumber account and associated data, please contact us using the email below. Email: onenumber.application@gmail.com

How to request deletion

  1. Email us from the address associated with your account
  2. Include the subject line: Data Deletion Request

What data is deleted

  • User account information
  • Associated app usage data
  • Authentication identifiers

What data may be retained

Certain data may be retained if required for legal, financial, or fraud-prevention purposes.
